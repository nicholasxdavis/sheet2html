Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This policy explains how sheet2html uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use them to remember your preferences and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We do not use any first-party cookies. This website stores no information on your device directly.

However, we use Google services (Google Sign-In and Google Sheets API) which may place third-party cookies on your device to facilitate authentication and data retrieval. These are controlled entirely by Google.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Blocking third-party cookies may prevent you from using the "Connect" feature, but "Flash Mode" will likely still function.
