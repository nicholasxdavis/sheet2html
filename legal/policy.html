Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At sheet2html, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Processing

Our service primarily processes data client-side in your browser. When you use Flash mode, your Google Sheet data is fetched directly by your browser and is not stored on our servers.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information unless explicitly provided by you (e.g., if you contact support). We may use anonymous analytics to improve our service.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google APIs to fetch spreadsheet data. By using this service, you also acknowledge Google's privacy practices.
